Email Infrastructure & Deliverability Optimization

Secure, compliant, and high-deliverability mail systems for business communication

Client Overview

A growing organization faced persistent mail delivery failures, spam flagging, and unreliable mail performance across multiple domains. The client required a stable, secure, and compliant mail infrastructure to support transactional and business-critical communication.

Challenges

  • Messages landing in spam or being rejected
  • Misconfigured SPF, DKIM, and DMARC records
  • Unstable mail server performance and storage issues
  • Lack of monitoring and visibility into mail flow
  • Poor sender reputation and inconsistent authentication

Solution Delivered

  • Audited complete mail infrastructure and DNS configuration
  • Implemented SPF, DKIM, DMARC, and BIMI correctly
  • Optimized SMTP routing and mail server performance
  • Secured mail services with TLS, rate limiting, and anti-abuse controls
  • Configured monitoring and alerting for mail delivery issues
  • Provided best-practice guidance for ongoing sender reputation

Technologies Used

SMTP, DNS, SPF, DKIM, DMARC, BIMI, Postfix, Exim, Zimbra, cPanel Mail, Mail Security & Monitoring

Outcome

  • Significant improvement in inbox placement
  • Stable and reliable mail delivery across providers
  • Improved sender reputation and trust
  • Reduced mail failures and user complaints
  • Secure and compliant mail infrastructure
